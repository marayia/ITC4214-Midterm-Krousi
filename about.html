About Us

Our Story

KMC Studios was born out of a shared passion for gaming and storytelling. We are a group of students from Athens, Greece, to put our creativity into action. Our journey started in 2025, with the release of our first game, Christmas Carol: Ghost of the Past, which was met with great enthusiasm. Since then, we have been dedicated to coding and designing games that we feel passionate about, and that resonate with others.

Meet the Team

Our team is made up of talented individuals who bring their unique skills and perspectives to the table. From game design and programming to art and storytelling, we work collaboratively to create immersive gaming experiences. We are committed to fostering a positive and inclusive work environment where creativity can thrive.
