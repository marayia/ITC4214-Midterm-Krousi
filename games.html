Our Games

We specialize in pixel graphics and use Pygame & Unity for all our projects. From action-packed platformers to narrative-driven adventures, our games are available on Steam, itch.io, and GameJolt — each one crafted with passion and a commitment to rich storytelling and engaging gameplay.

Rolling Knight

Rolling Knight is a fast-paced pixel-art platformer where you play as a knight who can only move by rolling. Navigate treacherous dungeons, defeat enemies, and master momentum-based movement to conquer each level.

Christmas Carol: Ghost of The Past

Christmas Carol: Ghost of The Past is a narrative-driven pixel-art adventure inspired by the classic Dickens story. Follow Ebenezer Scrooge through haunting visions of his past in this emotional and atmospheric short game filled with puzzles and narrative alternatives with a fresh twist.
